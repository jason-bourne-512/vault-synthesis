Recurring Emotional Loops

Loop 01 — Elevation-Fragmentation
Grand synthesis → vision is clear → distance from reality felt in full → interior collapse → distraction → recovery → grand synthesis again
Neither state is fake. The elevation keeps bringing the same clearing: "this is what you want, and you don't have it yet." The fragmentation is the descent from that clearing. The designed structure must survive both poles.
Loop 02 — Plan-Reset Cycle
Ambitious planning → motivated start → inconsistent follow-through → guilt + frustration → fragmentation → new plan → repeat
Plans produce the feeling of progress without behavioral change. A new plan gives the illusion of a restart without changing the structural condition. "The person who designs their daily routine goes further than the person who negotiates."
Loop 03 — The Tribeless Condition (8 years)
Identify community need → identify specific solution (parish, BJJ, Catholic young adults) → analysis without action → isolation deepens → re-identify same solution → repeat
Not a logistics problem. Community requires visible wanting, which creates the possibility of rejection. The "complete thinker" position (standing apart from all tribes) is simultaneously a protection. You can't be rejected by a group you haven't joined.
Loop 04 — Lust as Structural Relief
Stress / fight-or-flight elevation → fragmentation state → lust as interruption of unbearable longing → guilt → spiritual backslide → more stress
"I look to sins of the flesh to provide relief from the chaos then I wonder why I am in such pain." (March 2023) The lust is not simple desire — it is interruption of a longing that, without interruption, would be unbearable. The structural fix is reducing the longing's conditions (community, practice), not white-knuckling the behavior.
